## Bootleg Unit.

The bootleg unit has two inputs extra inputs for each player. p1, p2. This will be for low punch (LP) and low kick (LK).


### Control panel overview
Top-view of buttons on control panel. P1 and P2 are equivalent:

	S

 D      HP  HK   
R L       BL
 U      LP  LK

   R
